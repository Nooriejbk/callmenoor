Current Update

My June update is the update I'm the most proud of at the minute. This is the start of CallMeNoor at it's fullest.

In the previous update I wanted to tackle: continue button responsivness, put stuff in the portfolio and an update section for my progress.

I finally fixed the responsivness of my continue button, it took so long to work out that the span tag doesn't like percentage sizing... I hate working with pxl sizing so but I had to bite the bullet for this. With this I also fixed some general bugs like sizing or wrong colours.

I don't know what I was thinking before but the spotlight like was always a perfect thing to put in the portfolio and for whatever reason it took me 2 months to get to that conclusion.

And last but definitely not least, the amazing update section! For a small period of time I was stuck between having a slideshow style section with all the changes or a blog. I've made slideshows before and making a blog felt like a lot of extra effort for what it was worth. I do still like the idea of having a blog but I just don't have many ideas for content.

For my next update I want to focus on: add a new item to portfolio; an opening slide to the updates and add a navigation bar.

May Update

This was my first update for the website. I didn't want to throw myself into the deep end and instead decided to tackle a few things at a time.

In the previous update I wanted to tackle: prettier continue button; more content; nicer footer.

The continue button was an easy fix I just overthought, honestly. With the content, I made a list of things I want and things I can do and believe it or not, there were no overlap...yeah. But I knew I wanted a place to display all of my work and I thought I can make space for that as if I'm telling people what my goals are so I could be held accountable for doing it. Oddly, the welcome section was a last minute decision to beef up the site and I've grown to it, so I think it's gonna stay. I got inspiration for the footer from several websites I visited but with a more minimal style as I don't actually have anything to put there.

For my next update I want to focus on: responsivness of elements and put something in the portfolio, it looks quite sad at the minute. I also want a place to store my progress with the site, maybe an update section (inception) or incorporate a blog some how.
